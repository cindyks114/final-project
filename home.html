About Us

Second Time Around is a high end fashion consignment store in Toronto, Canada since 2005. We give consignors an easy way to monetize the amazing items in their closets, while fashion lovers can build their wardrobes. We believe that buying and selling pre-owned designer clothing should always be a great experience: seamless, personal, rewarding, and, of course, thrilling.

Consignment

Consignment isn’t just great for you, it’s great for the environment. It reduces your carbon footprint and refreshes your wardrobe, all while saving you money.

Second Time Around is committed to accepting only authentic items to re-sell to our customers. We believe in regularly educating our staff with up-to-date information on the ever-changing black market so they can quickly detect inauthentic elements, right down to the crooked top-stitch or the specific color of the tarnish on hardware.
